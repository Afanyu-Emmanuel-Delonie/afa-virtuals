Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with a virtual assistant

How does the 1-week trial period work?

The trial period allows you to experience my services with minimal risk. We'll define a small project or set of tasks, and I'll complete them to demonstrate my capabilities. If you're satisfied, we can continue working together. If not, there's no obligation to continue.

How do we communicate during projects?

Communication is key to successful collaboration. We can use your preferred platform (Slack, Email, Zoom, etc.) for regular updates. I provide progress reports and am available for scheduled check-ins to ensure everything is on track.

What are your working hours?

I typically work Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. However, I'm flexible and can adjust my schedule to accommodate different time zones or urgent tasks. For ongoing clients, I ensure availability during their core business hours.

How do you handle confidential information?

I take confidentiality very seriously. All client information is kept strictly confidential, and I'm happy to sign an NDA before starting work. I use secure, password-protected systems and follow best practices for data security.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, and major credit cards. Payment terms are typically monthly retainers or project-based, with invoices sent at the beginning of each month or upon project completion.

Do you offer website maintenance after design?

Yes, I offer ongoing website maintenance services to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally. This includes regular updates, backups, security monitoring, and content updates as needed.
