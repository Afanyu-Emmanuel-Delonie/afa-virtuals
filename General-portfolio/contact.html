Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to help you understand how we work, scale, and support your growth

How soon can we get started?

Once your discovery form is submitted and your onboarding is complete, we can typically begin within 24–72 hours.

Do you only work with certain industries?

We specialize in supporting coaches, creatives, founders, and small agencies but we’re open to working with any brand that values systems and support.

What services do you manage in-house?

All core services admin, social media, design, and web updates are handled by our internal team. When needed, we delegate to vetted experts under our supervision.

How do you ensure high-quality, consistent results?

We follow a Scalable Business Operating System (SBOS) that includes SOPs, internal reviews, client check-ins, and task tracking for every project.

Can I retain your team for ongoing support?

Yes! We offer flexible monthly support packages built to grow with your business from startup to scale.

What makes Afa-Virtuals different from a typical VA agency?

We don't just assign you a VA we become your remote support team, with systems, strategy, and creative execution that grows with you.
